Founder, Intervallum Labs

I've spent more than two decades working in corporate IT — the kind of environment where Microsoft 365 rollouts come with a dedicated project team, a change management consultant, and six months of user training. I know what good M365 adoption looks like when it's fully resourced.

The problem is, that's never the reality for small business owners. You're handed a subscription, pointed at a login page, and expected to figure the rest out yourself. Maybe you've got Outlook and Teams working. But the rest of your subscription — Power Automate, SharePoint, Copilot, Planner, Bookings, and a dozen other tools — is sitting dormant, costing you money every single month.

Intervallum Labs exists to close that gap. No jargon. No assuming you have an IT department. No enterprise-scale playbooks that don't translate to a 10-person company. Just practical, honest guidance built for the way small businesses actually work.
